About Us

Welcome to Posiria Beverages, your gateway to the vibrant world of Kerala's traditional snacks. Rooted in heritage and crafted with love, we bring to your table the rich and diverse flavors of South India. From the irresistible crunch of banana chips to the spicy tang of tomato murukku, our snacks are a celebration of culture and taste.

Kerala's culinary traditions are deeply tied to its lush landscapes and vibrant festivals. At Posiria Beverages, we embrace these roots, offering snacks that are not only delicious but also embody the spirit of togetherness and joy. Every bite tells a story — the aroma of freshly fried banana chips during Onam, the festive crunch of mixtures at weddings, or the savory bliss of murukku during evening chai sessions.

We take pride in using authentic recipes passed down through generations, ensuring that every product is as close to home as it can get. Our mission is to bring you premium-quality snacks that reflect the traditions of Kerala and South India, perfect for any occasion — whether it’s a festival, a casual gathering, or your quiet moments of indulgence.

At Posiria Beverages, we aren’t just about snacks; we’re about preserving a legacy, one savory bite at a time. Join us on this journey and discover the joy of authentic South Indian flavors!

Festive Features

Kerala’s festivals are incomplete without the perfect snacks. At Posiria Beverages, we provide the following:

  • Onam Special: Banana chips and Payyoli Mixture for the grand feast.
  • Diwali Treats: Crunchy murukku and spicy mixtures to light up your celebrations.
  • Wedding Delights: Sweet and savory snacks to add a touch of Kerala magic to your special day.

Let us make your celebrations extra special with our authentic and premium-quality snacks.
